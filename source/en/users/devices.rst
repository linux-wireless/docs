Looking for ...
***************

Are you looking for a specific device with specific capabilities? Unfortunately
we don't have all information easily accessible yet, but if you're, for
instance, looking for a card supporting AP mode, head over to the :doc:`drivers`
page, look at drivers that support AP mode, and then filter on those drivers on
these device pages.
