

===== iwl4965 =====

Intel maintains the iwl4965 driver. Their Linux driver project site is: 

[[http://www.intellinuxwireless.org/|http://www.intellinuxwireless.org/]] 

 


===== firmware =====

You can get the firmware for iwl4965 from: 

[[http://www.intellinuxwireless.org/?p=iwlwifi&n=downloads|http://www.intellinuxwireless.org/?p=iwlwifi&n=downloads]]
