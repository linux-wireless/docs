wlags49_h2
==========

This is a driver for WL60010,Hermes-II, and WL60040,Hermes-II.5,
Lucent/Agere chips.

They are included in the **staging** tree at linux-2.6/drivers/staging
and they are **NOT supported** by linux-wireless developers or mailing
list.

Instead ask for support in
http://driverdev.linuxdriverproject.org/mailman/listinfo/driverdev-devel
or to the maintainers Greg Kroah-Hartman <greg@kroah.com> and Henk de
Groot <pe1dnn@amsat.org>.
