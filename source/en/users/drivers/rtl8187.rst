rtl818x
-------

The rtl818x drivers provide support for 802.11a/b/g wireless chipsets from Realtek

Supported Chips
---------------

-  **rtl8180** PCI driver:

   -  RTL8180
   -  RTL8185
   -  RTL8187SE (**3.15+**)

-  **rtl8187** USB driver:

   -  RTL8187
   -  RTL8187B
