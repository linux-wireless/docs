

===== rt2800pci =====

Driver for [[http://www.mediatek.com|Ralink/MediaTek]] PCI devices RT2760, RT2790, RT2860, RT2880, RT2890 & RT3052. 

 


===== available devices =====

Please see the [[en/users/Drivers/rt2800pci/devices|device list]]. 
  * [[http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Hardware|http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/wiki/index.php/Hardware]] List of devices from the project site. 


===== Unsupported chips =====
    * [[http://www.mediatek.com/en/products/connectivity/wi-fi-new/pcnb/pcie/rt5592/|RT5592]], 802.11a/b/g/n 2T2R 2.4/5 GHz PCI Express Single Chip. (**WIP** Stanislaw Gruszka <[[mailto:stf_xl@wp.pl|stf_xl@wp.pl]]> : [[http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/pipermail/users_rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/2013-March/005765.html|http://rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/pipermail/users_rt2x00.serialmonkey.com/2013-March/005765.html]]) 

The Ralink driver for RT5592 can be downloaded from: [[http://www.asus.com/Networking/PCEN53/HelpDesk_Download/|http://www.asus.com/Networking/PCEN53/HelpDesk_Download/]] (direct link: [[http://dlcdnet.asus.com/pub/ASUS/wireless/PCE-N53/Linux_PCE_N53_1008.zip|http://dlcdnet.asus.com/pub/ASUS/wireless/PCE-N53/Linux_PCE_N53_1008.zip]] ) 
      * **MT7630E** 802.11b/g/n 1T1R 2.4 GHz Combo Card (Wireless PCI Express Minicard Module). (**WIP** Larry Finger <[[mailto:Larry.Finger@lwfinger.net|Larry.Finger@lwfinger.net]]> : [[http://github.com/lwfinger/mt7630|http://github.com/lwfinger/mt7630]]) 
      * **MT7662E/MT7612E** 802.11a/b/g/n/ac  2T2R 2.4/5 GHz PCI Express Single Chip (**WIP** Felix Fietkau <[[mailto:nbd@openwrt.org|nbd@openwrt.org]]> :[[https://github.com/openwrt/mt76|https://github.com/openwrt/mt76]]) 
      * [[https://wikidevi.com/wiki/MediaTek|Any new MediaTek chip]]. MediaTek drivers can be downloaded from: [[http://www.mediatek.com/en/downloads/|http://www.mediatek.com/en/downloads/]]
