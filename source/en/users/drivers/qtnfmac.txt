===== Introduction =====

**'qtnfmac**' is the [[en/developers/Documentation/Glossary|FullMAC]] wireless driver for Quantenna QSR10g chipset.

===== Features =====

  * AP mode, requires wpa_supplicant/hostapd 2.6 or newer
  * STA mode
  * 802.11bgn
  * 802.11ac


===== Status =====

This wireless driver has been accepted to mainline Linux v4.13. Driver is under active development.
