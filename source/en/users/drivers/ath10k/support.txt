===== Reporting bugs =====

Please send bug reports to the mailing list:

<ath10k@lists.infradead.org>

Please refrain from sending email for developers privately, they don't usually [[en/users/drivers/ath10k/privatesupport|have time for that]].

===== Mailing list =====

ath10k has it's own mailing list for discussions and patches to which anyone can subscribe to:

http://lists.infradead.org/mailman/listinfo/ath10k

Archive for the mailing list:

http://lists.infradead.org/pipermail/ath10k/

===== IRC channel =====

For real time discussions there is #ath10k channel on Freenode. Please do take into account that due to timezone differences and whatnot it might take hours, or even days, to get a reply. So patience is gold here.
