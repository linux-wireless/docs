





===== Subscribing to linux-wireless =====

To subscribe, send an an e-mail to // [[mailto:majordomo@vger.kernel.org|majordomo@vger.kernel.org]] // with anything on the subject, but on the body put: 


<code>subscribe linux-wireless</code>

===== Unsubscribing from linux-wireless =====

To unsubscribe, send an an e-mail to // [[mailto:majordomo@vger.kernel.org|majordomo@vger.kernel.org]] // with anything on the subject, but on the body put: 


<code>unsubscribe linux-wireless</code>

===== Additional mailing lists =====

Each driver //may// have its own specific mailing list. If you want to help hack on a driver please subscribe to that driver's respective mailing list. Additionally general Linux network development has its own mailing list, // [[mailto:netdev@vger.kernel.org|netdev@vger.kernel.org]] //. No subscription is required for posting to this mailing list. You can subscribe to this mailing list if you'd like to review general networking patches and work but please note that it is of considerably higher traffic than linux-wireless. 

To subscribe to netdev send an an e-mail to // [[mailto:majordomo@vger.kernel.org|majordomo@vger.kernel.org]] // with anything on the subject, but on the body put: 


<code>subscribe netdev</code>
